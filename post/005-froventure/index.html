Froventure

A platform game of a cute frog.

 Posted on April 20, 2023

It’s a set of tracks for a 2D platform game.

What are these for

These tracks are for the Lv. 99 Game Jam 2023.

I was so busy with other work and rarely participate in game design. So, I have little knowledge of what the game is, how it’s played, and others. I just knew it was about a cute frog jumping around the world.

The album is generated by AI, with

  • Prompt: cute frog in a pond, ambient light, 2D, anime, highly detailed
  • Steps: 20
  • Sampler: DPM++ SDE
  • CFG scale: 7
  • Seed: 2804447082
  • Size: 512x512
  • Model: Deliberate_v2
  • Denoising strength: 0.75

About the game and the tracks

I was asked for two tracks

  • One for the tutorial.
  • One for the game level.

Both are required to be

  • Calm
  • Acoustic
  • With a little interest

Download Looped music

These are prepared for Unity:

  • 01. Frog in the Pond - Intro
  • 01. Frog in the Pond - Loop
  • 02. Froventure - Intro
  • 02. Froventure - Loop

Design of music

01. Frog in the Pond is for the tutorial:

  • A piano is used for the melody part.
  • In the second section, the melody is handled by a set of violins. To add some interest, a flute comes in from time to time.
  • Two pads and a double base are used for the harmony part.

Then comes the music of the game: 02. Froventure

  • This one is supposed to be more upbeat than the first one.
  • Tempo is increased from 90 to 98.
  • The rest is similar to the first one. Nothing special to mention
